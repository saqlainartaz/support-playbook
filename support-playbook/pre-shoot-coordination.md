# Pre-Shoot Coordination

Use this when a customer wants to change, move, or cancel a studio session — or needs help
booking one. This is the most common kind of email we get.

---

## Situation 1 — "I need to change / move / cancel my studio session"

**What they usually say:** Something came up — travel trouble, an accident, illness, a clash —
and they need to push their session, bring it earlier, or cancel and rebook later.

**What to do:**
1. Say a quick, kind "sorry to hear that."
2. Send them the self-serve link so they can pick a new time themselves.

**Reply you can send:**
> Hi [their first name],
> Sorry to hear that — you can use this link to reschedule your session:
> go.oncehub.com/inside-success
> Thank you,
> [Your name]
> Client Concierge Manager

**Watch out for:**
- The **same link handles everything** — reschedule, moving earlier, or cancel-and-rebook. They
  choose the new time.
- If they say the link won't open, point them to a different browser or device.
- If their date change affects a shoot or travel that's already booked, give the production /
  logistics team a heads-up so nothing slips.

---

## Situation 2 — "How do I book my session?" / "Can you confirm my booking?"

**What to do:**
- Send the same booking link (`go.oncehub.com/inside-success`) so they can book themselves, and
  confirm the details once they're booked. The calendar sends its own reminders.

**Watch out for:**
- If they're asking how to **prepare** ("what do I bring?"), share the *Pre-shoot checklist* from
  the Help Center, and the B-roll guidance (see
  [B-Roll & Footage Submission](b-roll-and-footage-submission.md)).
- If they haven't actually been cast yet, that's a casting question — see
  [Casting Inquiries](casting-inquiries.md).
