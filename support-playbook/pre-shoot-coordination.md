# Pre-Shoot Coordination

Use this when a customer wants to change, move, or cancel a studio session — or needs help
booking one. This is the most common kind of email we get.

---

## Situation 1 — "I need to change / move / cancel my studio session"

**What they usually say:** Something came up — travel trouble, an accident, illness, a clash —
and they need to push their session, bring it earlier, or cancel and rebook later.

**What to do:**
1. Say a quick, kind "sorry to hear that."
2. Their reschedule/cancel link is **specific to their booking** — it is *not* the general
   `go.oncehub.com/inside-success` link (that one only books a brand-new session).
3. To get their link: search the client's name in the **studio Google Calendar**, open their
   booking, and copy the reschedule/cancel link — **or** tell the client to open their original
   **booking confirmation email**, where the same link lives.
4. Send them the link, or point them to their confirmation email.

**Replies you can send:**

*Point them to their email*
> Hi [their first name],
> Sorry to hear that — the link to reschedule or cancel your session is in your original booking
> confirmation email. Open that email and use the reschedule/cancel link inside.
> Thank you,
> [Your name]
> Client Concierge Manager

*Paste their link*
> Hi [their first name],
> Sorry to hear that — here's your link to reschedule or cancel your session:
> [paste their reschedule/cancel link]
> Thank you,
> [Your name]
> Client Concierge Manager

*Both in one reply*
> Hi [their first name],
> Sorry to hear that — you can reschedule or cancel your session using the link in your original
> booking confirmation email, or use this one directly: [paste their reschedule/cancel link]
> Thank you,
> [Your name]
> Client Concierge Manager

**Watch out for:**
- The reschedule/cancel link handles both — moving the session (earlier or later) and
  cancel-and-rebook.
- If they say the link won't open, point them to a different browser or device.
- If their date change affects a shoot or travel that's already booked, give the production /
  logistics team a heads-up so nothing slips.

---

## Situation 2 — "How do I book my session?" / "Can you confirm my booking?"

**What to do:**
- Send the same booking link (`go.oncehub.com/inside-success`) so they can book themselves, and
  confirm the details once they're booked. The calendar sends its own reminders.

**Watch out for:**
- If they're asking how to **prepare** ("what do I bring?"), share the *Pre-shoot checklist* from
  the Help Center, and the B-roll guidance (see
  [B-Roll & Footage Submission](b-roll-and-footage-submission.md)).
- If they haven't actually been cast yet, that's a casting question — see
  [Casting Inquiries](casting-inquiries.md).

---

## Situation 3 — "What's next?" / "Anything missing on my end?"

**What they usually say:** They want to know where they are in the process — what they still need
to do, or whether anything's outstanding on their side.

**What to do:**
1. Open the **Cast Fulfillment board** and check these columns for the client: **Contract**,
   **OB Form**, **OB Call**, **Script/Production Plan**, and **Shoot Date**.
2. If everything's complete, they're **good to go** — use the *All done* reply (waiting for their
   shoot, or for us to send the episode if they've already filmed).
3. If something's missing, send the matching reply with the link to complete it — *OB Call*,
   *OB Form*, *Script*, or *Shoot date*.
4. Only send the *Missing — Shoot date* reply once the **OB Form and OB Call are both complete**
   (the script doesn't need to be done first).

**Replies you can send:**

*All done — good to go*
> Hi [their first name],
> Great news — you've completed all of your onboarding tasks, so there's nothing else needed on
> your end right now! [Just sit tight for your upcoming shoot day. / Since you've already filmed,
> you're all set — just wait for us to send your episode over to you.]
> Thank you,
> [Your name]
> Client Concierge Manager

*Missing — OB Call*
> Hi [their first name],
> It looks like you still haven't attended an onboarding call. This group call gives you clarity on
> our process and answers a lot of common questions. Here's the link to schedule:
> go.oncehub.com/isonboarding
> Thank you,
> [Your name]
> Client Concierge Manager

*Missing — OB Form*
> Hi [their first name],
> It looks like you still haven't completed your onboarding form — this is the most important step
> of the process. Please fill it out so our team can start working on your promotional assets and
> create your script. Here's the link:
> https://form.typeform.com/to/Fs6tcBfy
> Thank you,
> [Your name]
> Client Concierge Manager

*Missing — Script*
> Hi [their first name],
> Our team is working on your script based on all the info you provided. We'll send it over as soon
> as it's complete — besides that, you're good to go!
> Thank you,
> [Your name]
> Client Concierge Manager

*Missing — Shoot date* (only once OB Form **and** OB Call are complete)
> Hi [their first name],
> It looks like you just need to schedule your studio session in Miami! Here's the link:
> go.oncehub.com/inside-success
> Thank you,
> [Your name]
> Client Concierge Manager

**Watch out for:**
- Only send the *Missing — Shoot date* reply once the OB Form and OB Call are both complete — the
  script doesn't need to be done yet.
- The OB Call schedule, OB Form, and studio booking links all live in the README's
  *Links & resources* section if you need them.
