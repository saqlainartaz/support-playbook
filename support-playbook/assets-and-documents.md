# Assets & Documents

Use this when a customer asks for a copy of a contract, agreement, or other document or asset.

---

## Situation — "Can I get a copy of my contract / a document?"

**What they usually say:** The customer (or their assistant) wants a copy of their signed
contract or another document — often ahead of a shoot or just for their records.

**What to do:**
1. Find the document — for a contract, go to the **Cast Fulfillment board**, search their name,
   find the contract column, and download it.
2. Attach it and send a short, friendly note.
3. CC the right internal person (e.g. the account or legal contact) so they know it went out.

**Reply you can send:**
> Hi [their first name],
> Attached is the [contract]!
> Thanks,
> [Your name]
> Client Concierge Manager

**Watch out for:**
- For sensitive documents, make sure the person asking is allowed to have it (for example, an
  assistant acting for the client). If you're not sure, check with the account owner first.
- For design or deliverable files (posters, thumbnails, landing pages, production plans), ask the
  design / production owner if you don't already have the latest version.
