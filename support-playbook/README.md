# Inside Success TV — Support Playbook

Your go-to guide for answering customer emails quickly and consistently. Find the situation,
follow the steps, and send the ready-made reply.

> **👉 Day-to-day use: open [`index.html`](index.html) in your browser.**
> It's the same content as the files below, but searchable, with category buttons and a one-click
> **Copy** button on every reply. Just double-click the file (or open the shared link). The `.md`
> files below are the editable source — update both if you change wording.

## How to use this playbook
1. **Find the category** that matches the email you're looking at.
2. **Match the situation** inside that file.
3. **Copy the reply**, swap in the details in `[brackets]`, and send.
4. **Check "Watch out for"** before sending — especially for anything about money or anything
   that isn't really our team's call.

## How we sound
- Warm, short, and human. Greet people by first name.
- If someone's upset, **lead with empathy** before anything else.
- Sign off as **`[Your name], Client Concierge Manager`** (or just "Best,").
- **Stay in your lane.** If it's a casting decision, scripting, or a production detail, loop in
  that team and tell the customer you've done so — don't guess.
