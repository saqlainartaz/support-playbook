# Money Mondays & Calls

Use this when a customer needs help joining the weekly live call ("Money Monday") or can't get
the link to work.

**Good to know:** Money Monday goes live **every Monday at 11:30am EST**. The join link is
**`insidesuccesstv.com/mondaylive`**.

---

## Situation — "I can't join the Monday call / I need the link"

**What they usually say:** They're trying to join and either need the link or are hitting a
connection error (like "DNS points to prohibited IP").

**What to do:**
1. Send the join link.
2. If they're getting an error, walk them through the quick fixes.

**Reply you can send:**
> Hi [their first name],
> The call link is working — here it is: insidesuccesstv.com/mondaylive
> If it won't open, try a different browser (Safari instead of Chrome, or the other way around),
> or switch between Wi-Fi and cellular data — that usually clears it up.
> Thank you,
> [Your name]
> Client Concierge Manager

**Watch out for:**
- For "can't connect" errors, the fixes are almost always: different browser, different device,
  or switch Wi-Fi ↔ cellular.
- For schedule or participation details, share the *Marketing and Promotion Intensive and event
  participation* article.
- For bigger events or masterminds (beyond the weekly call), point to that same article or loop
  in the events owner.
